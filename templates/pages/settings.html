This option allows you to adjust the transition effect for characters on the typing board only. Explanation: When you type any character and it starts showing the 'correct' or 'incorrect' color, a subtle animation will appear, making the typing experience smoother and more visually pleasing. Warning: Keep in mind that setting a high value in ms may cause the character color to appear delayed, which could make you think the program is slow.
